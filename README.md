Cloud backup moves your data to a remote server. You keep a copy of your files in a safe place. If your computer breaks or you lose your files, you can restore them from the cloud.

## 🔧 Frequently asked questions

**Where does the software store my backup files?**
You choose the destination during the setup process. You can store backups on a local drive, a network drive, or a cloud service like OneDrive or Google Drive.

**Does the software slow down my computer?**
The program runs in the background. It uses small amounts of system resources. You can adjust the performance settings if you notice an impact during heavy computer use.

**What happens if I lose my internet connection?**
The software pauses the backup task. It resumes the task once you reconnect to the network. Your progress remains safe.

**Can I restore single files?**
Yes. You can use the restore menu to pick specific files or folders. You do not need to restore the whole backup if you only need one or two missing files.

**Is my data safe during the upload?**
Yes. The software sends all data through an encrypted tunnel. This prevents outsiders from touching or reading your files while they travel across the internet.
